# resistor power calculator
 Written so i could calculate power to leds etc when using
raspberry pi gpio inputs at 5volt/3.3 volt

takes the input power ie 5 volt minus the led forward power
and divides by the constant 0.02. output is rounded to nearest
whole number in ohms for approximate resistor use.

      5 volt            -                 2  volt             /     0.02
main power      minus       led power        divide by

resistor = (5-2)/0.02  = approx 150 ohms
